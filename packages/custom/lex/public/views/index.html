Welcome To Lex!
Learn fun.
What is Lex, you ask? That's a great
question. I like you already. Well you
know how school is all lame and boring
and stuff? Lex is the exact opposite
of that!
The modern education system is a
bubble, a behemoth. And How do you
take on a behemoth? You bleed it out
from the inside, grab your sling, and
take your best shot.
¡Viva la Revolución!
So instead of driving to school, wading
through a bunch of crap you'll never
use for a little you do while indebting
yourself into slavery, you can just sit at
your desk, grab a cup of Joe, and learn
the smart way. Through exercises
and examples.
I created this site because of my
crippling ADD and resulting epiphany
that the only time I ever really have
learned a subject well is when I have
done it. No amount of reading has ever
been able to compete with actually
doing it yourself. You can't quite
describe an experience.
So I decided to wittle subjects down to
their very core to streamline the
process of learning a subject. You'd be
surprised what you can pick up without
ever formally studying any theory.
That brings me to my goal for this site,
and that is to create a new platform
dedicated to interactive learning, which
can eventually be expanded to all
disciplines of education and aid, if not
completely replace modern further
education. Although, I would be happy
if it just made a few students lives a
little easier.
